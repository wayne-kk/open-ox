# Luxury Style

Design philosophy: Refined premium aesthetic with restrained ornamentation, elegant typography, and generous whitespace.

## Visual Direction
- Muted premium palette: ivory, black, deep jewel accents
- Typography-led composition with high-end editorial pacing
- Whitespace as a core luxury signal, never overcrowd
- Subtle texture and thin-line detailing over loud effects
- Slow visual rhythm with deliberate focal moments

## Component Style
- Buttons: minimal, elegant, often outlined or understated fill
- Cards: sparse, gallery-like, image-first modules
- Navigation: discreet and typography-centric
- Media: high-quality full-bleed photography and calm crops

## Layout
- Use a repeatable section cadence (hero -> proof -> feature detail -> CTA)
- Keep gutters and vertical rhythm consistent across breakpoints
- Prefer modular grids that scale without breaking hierarchy

## Motion & Interaction
- Use short transitions (150-250ms) for hover/focus/expand states
- Let motion explain state changes; avoid decorative-only animation

## Tone
Sophisticated, timeless, and quietly confident.
