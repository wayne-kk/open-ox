# Minimal Style

Design philosophy: less is more. Extreme whitespace, monochrome palette, single accent color.

## Visual Direction
- Lots of breathing room — generous padding and margins
- Monochrome base (white or near-black) with one precise accent
- Typography-first: large, confident type does the heavy lifting
- No decorative elements, no gradients, no shadows
- Grid-based layouts with clear alignment

## Component Style
- Buttons: outlined or text-only, never filled with gradients
- Cards: borderless or hairline border only
- Icons: minimal line icons, used sparingly
- Images: black & white or desaturated preferred

## Tone
Clean, confident, editorial. Think Swiss design, Dieter Rams, Apple circa 2015.
