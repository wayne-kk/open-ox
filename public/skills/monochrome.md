# Monochrome Style

Design philosophy: A stark editorial system with pure black and white contrast, oversized serif hierarchy, and precise geometric composition.

## Visual Direction
- Pure black/white base; allow only grayscale neutrals for depth
- Oversized serif headlines with tight leading and high drama
- Hard edges, straight separators, and strict baseline rhythm
- Editorial spacing with deliberate negative space blocks
- No playful gradients; contrast is the primary visual engine

## Component Style
- Buttons: monochrome fill/invert states, no colorful accents
- Cards: magazine-like modules with sharp boundaries
- Navigation: minimal top rail, typographic emphasis over icons
- Imagery: high-contrast photography or monochrome duotone only

## Layout
- Use a repeatable section cadence (hero -> proof -> feature detail -> CTA)
- Keep gutters and vertical rhythm consistent across breakpoints
- Prefer modular grids that scale without breaking hierarchy

## Motion & Interaction
- Use short transitions (150-250ms) for hover/focus/expand states
- Let motion explain state changes; avoid decorative-only animation

## Tone
Austere, premium, fashion-editorial confidence.
