# Brutalist Style

Design philosophy: raw, honest, confrontational. No polish, no pretense — pure structure exposed.

## Visual Direction

- High contrast: black and white as primary palette, one jarring accent (red, yellow, or lime)
- Thick borders (2–4px solid black or white)
- No border-radius — everything is sharp rectangles
- Visible grid lines, exposed structure
- Monospace or grotesque fonts, sometimes mixed sizes chaotically
- Intentional "ugliness" as aesthetic choice

## Component Style

- Buttons: thick border, flat fill, no shadow, hover = invert colors
- Cards: bordered boxes, no shadow, stacked or offset
- Navigation: horizontal list, no icons, uppercase mono text
- Images: full bleed, no rounded corners, sometimes with visible captions

## Layout

- Asymmetric, intentionally off-balance
- Content can overflow or be tightly packed
- Visible column structure

## Tone

Raw, anti-design, provocative. Think early web, Craigslist-as-art, Balenciaga campaigns.
