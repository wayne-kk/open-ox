# Bold Style

Design philosophy: make a statement. High contrast, expressive typography, strong visual hierarchy.

## Visual Direction
- High contrast — dark backgrounds with vivid accent colors
- Oversized typography: hero text at 80–120px, tight leading
- Strong color blocking: full-bleed sections with distinct color zones
- Geometric shapes and hard edges
- Gradients used intentionally for depth, not decoration

## Component Style
- Buttons: solid fills, rounded or sharp corners, never subtle
- Cards: high-contrast borders or colored backgrounds
- Headlines: display fonts, heavy weight, sometimes outlined or gradient-filled
- Sections: alternating dark/light or color-blocked

## Tone
Energetic, confident, unapologetic. Think Stripe, Linear, Vercel's marketing pages.
