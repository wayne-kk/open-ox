# Glassmorphism Style

Design philosophy: depth through translucency. Frosted glass surfaces, layered blur, soft light.

## Visual Direction

- Dark or gradient background (deep navy, purple, or dark mesh gradient)
- Cards and panels: `backdrop-filter: blur(20px)`, semi-transparent backgrounds (`rgba(255,255,255,0.08)`)
- Subtle borders: `1px solid rgba(255,255,255,0.15)`
- Soft inner glow on interactive elements
- Layered depth: background → blur layer → content layer

## Component Style

- Cards: frosted glass with soft shadow and border glow
- Buttons: glass fill with hover glow effect
- Navigation: sticky glass bar with blur
- Modals/panels: heavy blur, light border, subtle shadow

## Color

- Background: deep dark gradient (e.g. `#0a0a1a` to `#1a0a2e`)
- Accent: electric blue, violet, or cyan
- Text: white with varying opacity for hierarchy

## Tone

Futuristic, premium, immersive. Think macOS Big Sur, iOS widgets, modern SaaS dashboards.
