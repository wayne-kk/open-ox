# Screen Skill: Community Profile Hub

Use this skill for social/community pages centered on identity, creator content, and interaction.

## Screen Intent

- Establish identity context quickly (profile header).
- Keep content and interactions tightly coupled.
- Encourage repeated engagement through concise modules.

## Recommended Regions

1. Profile summary area (avatar, name, short descriptor, follow/edit action).
2. Content switcher (posts, likes, collections, notes).
3. Primary content list/grid.
4. Social feedback row (followers, interactions, recent activity).

## Design Rules

- Keep profile area compact; avoid oversized hero portraits.
- Maintain consistent card/list style across tabs.
- Prefer short metadata and touch-friendly controls.
- Avoid website-like brand-story copy blocks.
