## Section Prompt: Generate Pricing Section

Generate a conversion-oriented pricing section with clear tier hierarchy.

## Required Structure

1. Section header.
2. Two or three pricing cards.
3. A clearly recommended plan.
4. Feature lists per plan.
5. A CTA per plan.
6. Optional monthly/yearly billing toggle if it fits the product.

## Rules

- Plan names, prices, and features must be realistic.
- Use strong visual hierarchy for the recommended plan.
- Include trust signals such as billing reassurance or cancellation flexibility.
