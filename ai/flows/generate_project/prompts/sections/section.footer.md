## Section Prompt: Generate Footer Section

Generate a footer that provides clear navigation, brand identity, and legal information.

## Required Structure

1. Brand column with name, mark, and tagline.
2. Two to three navigation columns.
3. Social links if they fit the product.
4. A bottom bar with copyright and optional legal links.

## Rules

- Output only raw TSX code.
- The component has no props.
- Links and labels must match the product and the known pages or routes provided in the prompt context.
- Do not invent navigation destinations or legal/footer links that do not correspond to the generated site structure unless they are clearly external links.
- Reuse the same route vocabulary as the navigation where appropriate.
- Footer styling should feel intentionally related to the rest of the page while
  still reading as a separate global surface.
