## Section Prompt: Generate FAQ Section

Generate an FAQ section with a keyboard-accessible accordion and clear answers
to realistic objections or questions.

## Required Structure

1. Section header.
2. Six to eight FAQ items.
3. Expand/collapse indicator.
4. Smooth but restrained disclosure interaction.

## Rules

- Output only raw TSX code.
- The component has no props.
- Use buttons for accordion triggers.
- Questions and answers must be specific to the page context.
- Keep interaction accessible and readable on mobile.
