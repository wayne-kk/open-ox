## Section Prompt: Generate Testimonials Section

Generate a testimonials section that builds trust through believable social proof.

## Required Structure

1. Section header.
2. Four to six testimonial cards.
3. Each card includes quote, reviewer identity, and role or company.
4. Optional aggregate rating badge or decorative quote treatment.

## Rules

- Output only raw TSX code.
- The component has no props.
- Testimonials must feel specific and authentic, not generic praise.
- Use visually varied but consistent card treatments.
- Avoid invalid JSX text such as unescaped quote characters in plain text nodes.
